# Sources & References

## Ancient Greek Sources

### Primary Texts
- Homer (~8th century BCE). *The Iliad*, Book 6. First written account of the Chimera.
- Hesiod (~8th century BCE). *Theogony*. Genealogy including Chimera's parentage (Typhon and Echidna).
- Apollodorus (~2nd century CE). *Bibliotheca* (Library). Detailed account of Bellerophon and the Chimera.

### Roman Sources
- Ovid (1st century BCE-1st century CE). *Metamorphoses*. Roman retelling including Chimera references.
- Virgil (1st century BCE). *Aeneid*. References to Greek monsters including the Chimera.
- Pliny the Elder (1st century CE). *Natural History*. Roman natural history encyclopedia.
